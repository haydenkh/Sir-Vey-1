Director Profile

Here is your home base. You have the option of creating a form,
choosing to whom you send your created form, and viewing the
results you've collected from your students.




This leads you to a form-creator page. You'll be able to write your question and provide either a text box response area or a selection of your preferred multiple choice answers.


This leads you to a list of your students. To select a student to send your survey to, simply check the box next to their name. If you'd like to send to all of your students, click "Select All".


This leads you to your students' answers. Here you'll find an organized layout of the results of your survey. You can compare your students' answers and refer back to them whenever.
